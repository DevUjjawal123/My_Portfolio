Event Hub Website

Description: I developed the Event Hub website, a comprehensive platform designed to list and showcase statewide events. This dynamic website provides users with detailed information about each event, including the time, location, and additional relevant details. The platform allows users to browse through various events, search for specific categories, and interact by booking tickets or saving events to their calendar. Placement Portal

Description: I created a dynamic Placement Portal website for KIET Group of Institutions, aimed at streamlining the placement process for students and recruiters. The website features a user-friendly interface that allows students to view and apply for job listings, build and customize their resumes using a built-in Resume Builder tool, and track their application statuses. It also includes detailed company profiles, resources for interview preparation, event listings, and collaboration opportunities. The portal is designed to enhance the overall placement experience, providing valuable tools and information to both students and recruiters.

Skills: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP
