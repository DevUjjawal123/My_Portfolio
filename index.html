Projects

Event Hub

Event Hub is a dynamic web application designed to keep you informed about upcoming events happening near you. Whether you're looking for concerts, conferences, workshops, or local gatherings, Event Hub uses real-time location data to provide you with a curated list of events tailored to your preferences. Key Features: Location-Based Search: Discover events happening in your vicinity. Time Filters: Easily find events based on your availability. Event Details: Get comprehensive information about each event, including time, location, and description. Personalized Recommendations: Tailored event suggestions based on your interests and past preferences. Event Hub is not just an event tracker; it's your gateway to staying connected and engaged with the vibrant events happening around you. Explore, discover, and never miss out on the next big thing with Event Hub.

Resume Builder

Resume Builder is an intuitive online tool designed to streamline the process of creating professional resumes. Whether you're a job seeker entering the workforce or a seasoned professional looking to update your CV, Resume Builder offers a user-friendly interface with robust features to craft polished resumes effortlessly. Key Features: Customizable Templates: Choose from a variety of professionally designed templates tailored to different industries and career stages. Easy Editing: Edit, format and personalize your resume with simple drag-and-drop Section Customization: Organize your skills, experience, education, and achievements with customizable sections. Preview and Download: View your resume in real-time as you make changes and instantly download it in PDF format. Cloud store: Save and access your resumes securely from anywhere.Resume Builder empowers users to create impactful resumes that stand out to potential employers, helping them showcase their qualifications and skills effectively.

Placement Portal

As part of my academic and professional development, I developed a comprehensive Placement Portal for the KIET Group of Institutions. This portal is designed to streamline the placement process for students, providing a user-friendly interface that connects them with potential employers. Key features include centralized job listings categorized by industry and role, an intuitive resume builder with professional templates, and application tracking to monitor job application statuses. Additionally, the portal offers detailed company profiles, interview preparation resources with common questions and mock sessions, and information about career-related events and workshops. Collaboration features allow students to share experiences and seek advice from peers and mentors, fostering a supportive community. The portal's minimalist design ensures a seamless user experience, with a creative vertical layout that organizes content effectively and uses images sparingly to maintain focus on essential information and functionality.
