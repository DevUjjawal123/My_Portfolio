Event Hub

Whether you're looking for concerts, conferences, workshops, or local gatherings, Event Hub uses real-time location data to provide you with a curated list of events tailored to your preferences. Key Features: Location-Based Search: Discover events happening in your vicinity. Time Filters: Easily find events based on your availability. Resume Builder

Resume Builder is an intuitive online tool designed to streamline the process of creating professional resumes. Whether you're a job seeker entering the workforce or a seasoned professional looking to update your CV, Resume Builder offers a user-friendly interface with robust features to craft polished resumes effortlessly. Key Features: Customizable Templates: Choose from a variety of professionally designed templates tailored to different industries and career stages.
